# Ebook Sources Reference

## Anna's Archive

- **URL**: https://annas-archive.org
- **Search**: https://annas-archive.org/search?q={query}
- **Notes**: Largest free ebook meta-index. Aggregates Library Genesis,
  Sci-Hub, and many other shadow libraries. Supports title, author, ISBN,
  language filters. Direct download links available after selecting a result.
  May show multiple mirror links per entry.
- **Formats**: PDF, EPUB, MOBI, AZW3, FB2, and more
- **CAPTCHA**: Occasionally present; skip if triggered

## Z-Library

- **URL**: https://z-lib.io (primary mirror; may change)
- **Search**: https://z-lib.io/s/{query}
- **Notes**: One of the largest ebook libraries. Wide format coverage.
  Mirrors change frequently — check the main domain first.
- **Formats**: PDF, EPUB, MOBI
- **CAPTCHA**: Rare but possible

## Library Genesis (LibGen)

- **URL**: http://libgen.rs (primary)
- **Search**: http://libgen.rs/search.php?req={query}
- **Notes**: Academic-focused, massive collection. Good for textbooks,
  scientific papers, and academic books. Search by title, author, or ISBN.
- **Formats**: PDF, EPUB, DJVU, MOBI
- **CAPTCHA**: Rare

## PDF Drive

- **URL**: https://www.pdfdrive.com
- **Search**: https://www.pdfdrive.com/search?q={query}
- **Notes**: Dedicated PDF search engine with a large index. Good for
  non-academic books and general reading.
- **Formats**: Primarily PDF
- **CAPTCHA**: Rare

## Internet Archive

- **URL**: https://archive.org
- **Search**: https://archive.org/search?query={query}&mediatype=texts
- **Notes**: Public domain books and scanned library copies. Many books
  available in multiple formats including EPUB, PDF, DAISY, and Kindle.
  Legally safe source.
- **Formats**: PDF, EPUB, MOBI, AZW3, DAISY, plain text
- **CAPTCHA**: No

## Google Books

- **URL**: https://books.google.com
- **Search**: https://www.google.com/search?q={query}+site:books.google.com
- **Notes**: Best for ISBN lookups and bibliographic info (year, publisher,
  edition). Preview only, not a download source. Use to verify book details
  before searching download sources.
- **Formats**: Preview only
- **CAPTCHA**: No

## Amazon Kindle / KDP

- **URL**: https://www.amazon.com/s?k={query}&i=digital-text
- **Notes**: Commercial platform. Use to check if a book exists and get
  accurate metadata (author, ISBN, year). Not a free download source.
- **CAPTCHA**: No

## B-OK (Z-Library related)

- **URL**: https://b-ok.cc
- **Search**: https://b-ok.cc/s/{query}
- **Notes**: Alternative Z-Library mirror. Same collection, different domain.
- **Formats**: PDF, EPUB, MOBI
- **CAPTCHA**: Rare

## EpubGo

- **URL**: https://epubgo.com
- **Search**: https://epubgo.com/search?q={query}
- **Notes**: Smaller but focused EPUB catalog. Good for fiction in EPUB format.
- **Formats**: EPUB, MOBI, PDF
- **CAPTCHA**: Rare

## ISO Republic / Free-Ebooks.net

- **URL**: https://www.free-ebooks.net
- **Search**: https://www.free-ebooks.net/search/{query}
- **Notes**: Mix of public domain and author-submitted free ebooks.
  Lower volume, higher curation.
- **Formats**: PDF, EPUB
- **CAPTCHA**: No

## Chinese Ebook Sources

### 书格 (ShuGe)

- **URL**: https://new.shuge.org
- **Notes**: Chinese public domain classical texts and scanned books.
  High-quality digitization of Chinese cultural heritage.

### 鸠摩搜书 (Jiumo)

- **URL**: https://www.jiumodiary.com
- **Notes**: Chinese ebook search engine. Aggregates links from multiple
  Chinese ebook sources.

### 苦瓜书盘

- **URL**: https://www.kgbook.com
- **Notes**: Chinese ebook community with EPUB and MOBI downloads.
